Privacy matters — here’s how we protect your information.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Dynamix Pro (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or contact us about our Ottawa wedding DJ services.

By using this website or providing your information to us, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of our website and services.

1. Introduction & Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through our website, contact forms, email communications, phone calls, and any other interactions you have with Dynamix Pro related to our DJ, MC, and lighting services for weddings and events in the Ottawa region.

For the purposes of applicable privacy laws, we act as the “data controller” for the personal information we collect and process about you. We are committed to handling your personal information in a lawful, fair, and transparent manner and in accordance with applicable Canadian privacy legislation.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, as well as certain technical information that is automatically collected when you use our website.

2.1 Information you provide directly

When you contact us, request a quote, or otherwise communicate with us, we may collect:

  • Name and contact details (such as email address and phone number)
  • Event details (such as wedding date, venue, approximate guest count, schedule)
  • Preferences and notes (such as music preferences, language preferences, special requests)
  • Any other information you choose to include in messages or forms

2.2 Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, certain information may be automatically collected by your browser or device, including:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent on pages, and referring/exit pages
  • Date and time of your visit and general website usage data

This type of information is typically collected using cookies and similar technologies, as described in the Cookie Policy section below.

2.3 Information from third parties

In some cases, we may receive information about you from third parties, such as:

  • Wedding planners, venues, or vendors who refer you to us or coordinate on your behalf
  • Platforms or directories where you contact us (for example, wedding listing sites)

We treat any information received from third parties in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes that are compatible with providing and improving our services, and only where we have a lawful basis to do so.

3.1 Service delivery and customer support

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes or proposals
  • Plan, coordinate, and deliver DJ, MC, and lighting services for your event
  • Communicate with you and/or your vendors before, during, and after your event
  • Manage bookings, contracts, and payments (where applicable)

3.2 Website operation and improvements

We may use automatically collected data to:

  • Operate, maintain, and secure our website
  • Monitor website performance and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Analyze overall usage trends to improve content, layout, and user experience

3.3 Marketing and communications

With your consent where required by law, we may use your contact details to:

  • Send you updates about our availability, services, or special information related to wedding entertainment
  • Follow up after an inquiry to ensure you received the information you needed

You may opt out of non-essential marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions (where provided) or by contacting us directly.

3.4 Legal and administrative purposes

We may also use your information where necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety and that of our clients and partners
  • Maintain accurate records for tax, accounting, or business operations

4. Sharing & Retention of Your Information

4.1 When we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in limited circumstances, including:

  • With service providers or tools that help us operate our website, manage inquiries, or deliver services (for example, email or form providers), subject to appropriate safeguards
  • With planners, venues, or vendors involved in your event, but only as reasonably necessary to coordinate services
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to respond to valid requests from public authorities

4.2 Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Factors that influence our retention periods include:

  • The duration of our relationship with you (for example, until after your wedding or event is completed)
  • Legal, tax, and accounting requirements to retain certain records
  • The existence of any disputes or potential disputes

When information is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to delete, anonymize, or securely destroy it.

5. Cookie Policy & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and understand how visitors interact with our content.

5.1 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences or pages you have viewed.

5.2 Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, such as basic security and page navigation.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are most popular), so we can improve our content and layout.

Any third‑party tools we use for analytics or performance will typically set their own cookies, which are subject to their respective privacy policies.

5.3 Managing cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality or performance of our website.

For more detailed guidance, refer to your browser’s help documentation or visit reputable resources that explain how to manage browser cookies.

6. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain rights regarding the personal information we hold about you. These can include the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact Us section below. We may need to verify your identity before responding to certain requests.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal obligations. When we make material changes, we will update the “last updated” date at the bottom of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, our privacy practices, or would like to exercise your rights, you can contact us at:

Email: info@dynamixpro.ca

Preferred Contact: Email communication for record-keeping and clarity.
